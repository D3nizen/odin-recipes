Cowboy Spaghetti

this dish will make you feel like you're in an old western!

Everyone will love this cowboy spaghetti, a fun twist with bacon, cheese, and pasta in a spicy beef and tomato sauce. The best part is that it’s a one-dish meal, cooked in a cast iron skillet.

ingredients

  • 6 strips bacon
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 8 ounces spaghetti noodles
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 3 green onions, sliced

Steps

  1. Place bacon in a large cast iron skillet and cook over medium-high heat, until slightly crisp. Crumble roughly and set aside.
  2. Add onion to the same skillet with bacon grease and sauté until softened. Add garlic, salt, and pepper. Stir until garlic is fragrant.
  3. Add ground beef. Cook and stir until beef is crumbly and no longer pink.
  4. Break spaghetti noodles in half. Scatter over ground beef, making sure noodles are separated. Pour tomatoes with green chiles, fire-roasted tomatoes, and tomato sauce over noodles.
  5. Remove the lid and stir to combine the pasta and sauce. Smooth out mixture and scatter shredded cheese evenly over top. Sprinkle with remaining bacon.
