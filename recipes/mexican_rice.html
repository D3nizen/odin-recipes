Mexican Rice

A simplified version of a delicious authentic dish

Mexican Rice is a simple dish that allows you to have a nutritious meal that doesn't break the bank! The ingredients are available anywhere and are easy to throw together to make an amazing meal that your family will love!

This dish will quickly become a staple of your weekly meals because of the simplicity!

ingredients

  • Oil
  • Rice
  • Seasonings
  • Onion
  • Broth
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Season the rice and cook in oil until puffed and golden.
  2. Add the onion and cook until the onion is softened.
  3. Stir in the broth and tomato sauce, then bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce to a simmer, cover, and cook for 20 more minutes.
